# 🌾 Community Service Project

## Rural Agriculture and Awareness Program

### 📍 Location

Komaragiripatnam Village, Andhra Pradesh

### 📅 Duration

March 1, 2026 – April 7, 2026

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Understand problems faced by farmers
* Conduct surveys and collect real data
* Provide awareness and solutions
* Improve agricultural practices

---

## 🛠 Activities Performed

* Conducted farmer surveys
* Identified issues like:

  * Low crop prices
  * Pest attacks
  * Irrigation problems
* Organized awareness campaigns
* Suggested solutions like:

  * Plantix app for pest control
  * Drip irrigation
  * Water management techniques

---

## 📊 Key Outcomes

* Farmers gained awareness about modern techniques
* Improved understanding of pest control
* Increased knowledge about water conservation
* Better interaction between students and community
